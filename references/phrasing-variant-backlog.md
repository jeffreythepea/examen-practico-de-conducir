## Standing exclusion rule

Variants that change the required response are prohibited. For example,
`¿Cómo se abre el capó?` would demand an opening demo, not the levels recital
`c-pre-capo` requires. It therefore cannot be added as a wording-only variant
of that command; it would need separate action and response-surface review.
